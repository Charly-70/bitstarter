We introduce you geobeat! This is a game based on geolocation, through which compete to gain the most spectacular locations, based on the challenge presented by a brand at a given time.

The game is focused on brands, so each week, a brand will offer a challenge to beat.

Place the product in the best beaches, the tallest buildings, the best coral reefs, or the most incredible subway!!

Just send your best photo with the product!

Each brand has its own sensitivity, will you be the one best able to interpret it?

Each week, players vote for the crowd favorite, and brand awarded to the best!

Each week, a new challenge!

Stay tuned to the latest challenges and compete around the world to defeat them all!
